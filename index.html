About Me

I hold an M.Sc. degree in Environmental Science from the Central Department of Environmental Science, Tribhuvan University. I am passionate about working in the field of environmental science, with a focus on disaster risk management and the application of Geographic Information Systems (GIS). I have the ability to quickly adapt to changing conditions and excel in multi-tasking research field activities, data collection, and presentation.

Currently, I am affiliated with the Centre of Research for Environment, Energy and Water (CREEW), a non-governmental, not-for-profit scientific research and development organization established in 2008. At CREEW, I have worked as a team member on various projects related to disaster risk reduction and water quality analysis. In the past, I have also worked as a consultant for conducting environmental assessments.
